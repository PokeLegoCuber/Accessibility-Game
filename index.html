What is Accessibilty Game?

The Accessibility Game is a game that aims to spreading awareness about the different kinds of accessibility and the importance that it plays in our society. The game gives you a general overview about accessibility, runs through some scenarios with the goal of teaching the player about different kinds of accessibilities.
